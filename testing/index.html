Therapy That Looks Beneath the Surface

Symptoms often make sense when you understand the nervous system, emotional patterns, attachment history, and protective strategies underneath them.

These services are designed to help you better understand what is happening inside, build more emotional steadiness, and move toward change that feels grounded rather than forced.

You can explore each service below, read more in the blog, or browse the resources page if you are still learning what kind of support may fit.

You may be looking for help with:

  • Anxiety, overthinking, panic, or nervous system overwhelm
  • Unresolved experiences, emotional triggers, or survival mode
  • Depression, numbness, low motivation, or feeling disconnected
  • Relationship patterns, boundaries, trust, or communication
  • Life transitions, stuckness, direction, confidence, or aligned action

Choose the Service Pathway That Feels Closest

Each service page includes more detail about what you may be experiencing, what therapy or coaching can support, what to expect, and related resources.

Anxiety Therapy

Support for chronic worry, panic, overthinking, uncertainty, tension, and feeling like your nervous system is always on.

Anxiety therapy can help you understand what keeps your internal alarm system activated, build regulation tools, and develop more confidence in your ability to handle uncertainty.

Explore Anxiety Therapy

Trauma Therapy

Trauma-informed support for unresolved experiences, emotional triggers, shame, survival responses, and feeling stuck in the past.

Trauma therapy can help you gently reconnect with your body, emotions, and inner world without forcing you to relive everything at once.

Explore Trauma Therapy

Depression Therapy

Support for emotional numbness, heaviness, low motivation, disconnection, self-criticism, and feeling like life has become muted.

Depression therapy is not about forcing positivity. It is about understanding shutdown, reducing shame, and reconnecting with meaning and energy at a sustainable pace.

Explore Depression Therapy

Relationship Counseling

Support for communication, emotional triggers, boundaries, attachment patterns, trust, conflict, and more secure connection.

Relationship counseling helps you understand the internal patterns that shape how you connect, protect yourself, express needs, and respond during conflict.

Explore Relationship Counseling

Life Coaching

Support for life transitions, identity shifts, motivation, follow-through, decision-making, clarity, confidence, and aligned action.

Life coaching offers a future-oriented space to clarify what matters, identify internal blocks, and create realistic steps toward a life that feels more like yours.

Explore Life Coaching
Not Sure Which Page to Choose?

Many Experiences Overlap

Anxiety can overlap with trauma. Depression can overlap with shutdown, exhaustion, or unresolved emotional pain. Relationship patterns can connect to attachment, nervous system responses, and past experiences.

You do not have to name everything perfectly before reaching out. The first step can simply be noticing that something in your life feels hard to carry alone.

FAQ

Questions About Services

Which therapy service is right for me?

The right service depends on what you are experiencing. Anxiety therapy may fit chronic worry or panic, trauma therapy may fit unresolved experiences or survival responses, depression therapy may fit heaviness or disconnection, relationship counseling may fit patterns in connection, and life coaching may fit clarity, direction, or transitions.

Do you offer online therapy in Florida?

Get Real Therapy supports clients in Fort Lauderdale and online across Florida, depending on fit, service needs, and consultation details.

Can I book a consultation if I am not sure what I need?

Yes. A consultation can help you share what is going on, ask questions, and begin identifying which support pathway may be the best fit.
