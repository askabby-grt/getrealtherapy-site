The Heart of the Work

You Are Not a Problem to Fix

Many people come to therapy feeling like something is wrong with them. They may feel anxious, shut down, reactive, disconnected, or stuck in patterns they cannot seem to change.

At Get Real Therapy, symptoms are not treated as random problems. They are understood as signals, adaptations, and protective responses that often make sense when we look at the nervous system, emotional history, relationships, and life context around them.

This work is about understanding what is happening beneath the surface so change can become more compassionate, more sustainable, and more connected to who you actually are.

This approach is built around:

  • Emotional safety
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Nervous system awareness
  • Attachment and relationship patterns
  • Practical tools for daily life
  • Deeper self-understanding
What Get Real Means

Honest, Human, Grounded Therapy

Get Real Therapy is about making space for what is actually happening, not the version of you that feels like it has to perform, explain perfectly, or hold everything together.

Real Patterns

We look at the patterns that keep showing up, including anxiety, avoidance, shutdown, overthinking, people-pleasing, perfectionism, or emotional reactivity.

Real Context

Your experiences are understood in context: your relationships, nervous system, history, identity, stress, and the ways you learned to protect yourself.

Real Change

The goal is not to force positivity or quick fixes. The goal is more clarity, more choice, more emotional steadiness, and a life that feels more like yours.

Clinical Style

A Deeper Approach Than Coping Skills Alone

Coping skills can be helpful, but they often are not enough when the deeper pattern has not been understood.

This work may include trauma-informed therapy, EMDR, Internal Family Systems, AEDP-informed emotional processing, attachment-based support, relationship work, nervous system regulation, and coaching strategies when appropriate.

The approach is reflective and practical. Some sessions may focus on understanding emotional patterns. Others may focus on processing, regulation, boundaries, decision-making, or concrete next steps.

What to Expect Who This Is For

The work may support:

  • Understanding triggers and emotional responses
  • Reducing shame and self-criticism
  • Building nervous system regulation
  • Processing unresolved experiences
  • Strengthening boundaries and communication
  • Taking aligned action in real life

FAQ

Questions About Get Real Therapy

What is Get Real Therapy?

Get Real Therapy offers trauma-informed therapy and coaching support for anxiety, trauma, depression, relationship patterns, life transitions, and meaningful personal change in Fort Lauderdale and online across Florida.

What kind of therapy approach is used?

The work is trauma-informed, attachment-aware, nervous-system focused, and may include EMDR, Internal Family Systems, AEDP-informed emotional processing, relationship work, and practical tools depending on your needs.

Who is this therapy practice for?

This practice is for people who may be high-functioning on the outside but feel anxious, stuck, disconnected, overwhelmed, emotionally tired, or ready to understand themselves more deeply.

Do I need to know what type of therapy I need before reaching out?

No. You do not need to know exactly what type of support you need before reaching out. A consultation can help clarify whether anxiety therapy, trauma therapy, depression therapy, relationship counseling, life coaching, or another path may fit.

Does Get Real Therapy offer online therapy?

Get Real Therapy supports clients in Fort Lauderdale and online across Florida, depending on fit, service needs, and consultation details.
