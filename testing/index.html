Why Relationships Can Feel So Hard Sometimes

Relationships are often where we feel the most connection—and the most distress.

You may notice patterns that feel difficult to explain or change, even when you are trying your best.

These experiences are often not random—they are shaped by deeper emotional and relational patterns.

  • Overthinking interactions or replaying conversations
  • Feeling anxious or unsure where you stand
  • Difficulty expressing needs or setting boundaries
  • Feeling reactive, hurt, or misunderstood
  • Repeating the same patterns in different relationships
  • Emotional exhaustion or disconnection

These Patterns Have a History

Many relational patterns develop over time as ways to stay connected, safe, or understood—even if they no longer feel helpful now.

Why Relationships Can Feel So Triggering

Anxiety

Seeking reassurance, overthinking, or fear of losing connection.

Avoidance

Pulling away, shutting down, or creating emotional distance.

Reactivity

Heightened emotions like anger, defensiveness, or sensitivity.

These responses are not flaws—they are protective patterns shaped by your past experiences. Learn more about who this work is for.

What Relationship Counseling Can Support

This work focuses on helping you understand your internal experience so you can show up differently in your relationships.

  • Understanding relational patterns
  • Reducing emotional reactivity
  • Building healthier communication
  • Strengthening boundaries
  • Processing past relational experiences
  • Increasing clarity and confidence
  • Creating more secure connections

Change Begins Internally

As your internal experience shifts, your relationships often begin to shift as well.

My Approach to Relationship Work

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

Understand the different parts of you that show up in relationships and learn how to respond with more clarity and balance.

EMDR Therapy

Process past relational experiences so they no longer feel emotionally charged in the present.

AEDP Therapy

Work with emotions in real time to move from reactivity or shutdown into connection and understanding.
