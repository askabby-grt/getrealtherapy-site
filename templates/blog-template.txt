--------------------------------------------------
2. SEO ELEMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

SEO title:
Keep under 60 characters.
Example:
Trauma Therapy in Fort Lauderdale | Get Real Therapy

Meta description:
Keep between 145 and 160 characters.
Example:
Learn how trauma therapy can help you feel safer, more grounded, and more in control. Get support from Get Real Therapy in Fort Lauderdale.

H1:
Use a natural, readable title that matches user intent.

--------------------------------------------------
3. ARTICLE STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------

INTRO
- Open with the emotional reality of what the reader may be feeling
- Use the primary keyword naturally
- Keep this useful, not dramatic
- Promise clarity or support

H2: WHAT THIS EXPERIENCE CAN LOOK LIKE
- Explain symptoms, patterns, or situations
- Reflect the reader's lived experience

H2: WHY IT HAPPENS
- Give psychoeducation in simple language
- Avoid jargon unless briefly explained

H2: HOW THERAPY CAN HELP
- Explain what support looks like
- Mention specific benefits
- Link to the most relevant service page

H2: WHAT TO DO NEXT
- Offer gentle guidance
- Include a soft CTA to book a consultation

FAQ SECTION
- Add 4 to 6 real search-style questions
- Use plain language
- Answer clearly and briefly

CONCLUSION
- Reassure the reader
- Repeat the main keyword naturally
- End with a soft invitation to reach out

--------------------------------------------------
4. INTERNAL LINKING RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Every article should include 3 to 5 internal links.

Always include:
- 1 link to the most relevant service page
- 1 link to /what-to-expect or /about
- 1 link to /contact or /book-consultation

Suggested service page targets:
- Anxiety-related article -> /anxiety-therapy
- Trauma-related article -> /trauma-therapy
- Depression-related article -> /depression-therapy
- Relationship-related article -> /relationship-counseling
- Self-growth or motivation article -> /life-coaching

Anchor text best practices:
- Keep anchor text natural
- Do not force exact-match anchors repeatedly
- Examples:
  "anxiety therapy"
  "what to expect in therapy"
  "book a consultation"
  "support for trauma recovery"

--------------------------------------------------
5. IMAGE GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------

Every blog post should have:
- 1 featured image
- 1 to 3 supporting images if needed

Image style:
- calming
- natural
- warm
- grounded
- human
- non-stock-looking when possible

Image SEO:
- descriptive filename
- descriptive alt text
- compressed image size
- no generic names like image1.jpg

Example:
Filename:
anxiety-therapy-fort-lauderdale.jpg

Alt text:
Woman sitting calmly during anxiety therapy session in Fort Lauderdale

--------------------------------------------------
6. CTA RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Use a soft CTA, not aggressive sales language.

Examples:
- If you are ready for support, book a consultation.
- You do not have to navigate this alone.
- If this feels familiar, therapy may help you move forward.

Mid-article CTA:
Keep it short and contextual.

End CTA:
Invite the reader to contact Get Real Therapy or book a consultation.

--------------------------------------------------
7. BLOG QUALITY CHECKLIST
--------------------------------------------------

Before publishing, confirm:
- Primary keyword appears naturally
- Meta title and description are written
- URL slug is clean
- H2s are clear and useful
- FAQ section is included
- 3 to 5 internal links are included
- Service page is supported
- CTA is present
- Tone is compassionate and clear
- No keyword stuffing
- No exaggerated claims
- No em dashes
