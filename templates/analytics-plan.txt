GET REAL THERAPY - GLOBAL ANALYTICS PLAN

Goal:
Create one global analytics layer that works across the entire site, then add page-specific event attributes to money pages, SEO pages, blog posts, forms, and CTAs.

Global GA4 Measurement ID:
G-8YW12523HN

GLOBAL EVENTS TRACKED SITEWIDE

1. site_page_view
Tracks every page view after the global JS loads.

Parameters:
- page_type
- service
- page_slug
- page_title
- primary_goal

2. scroll_depth
Tracks 25%, 50%, 75%, and 90% page scroll.

Parameters:
- scroll_percent
- page_type
- service
- page_slug

3. outbound_link_click
Tracks clicks to external URLs such as Instagram, YouTube, X, Spotify, etc.

Parameters:
- event_label
- link_url
- page_slug

4. form_start
Tracks when someone first interacts with a form.

Parameters:
- form_name
- page_type
- page_slug

5. form_validation_error
Tracks when a form is submitted with invalid or missing required fields.

Parameters:
- form_name
- invalid_field_count
- invalid_fields
- page_slug

6. form_submit_attempt
Tracks when a valid form submission is attempted.

Parameters:
- form_name
- page_slug

7. faq_view
Tracks when FAQ blocks enter the viewport.

Parameters:
- faq_question
- page_slug

PAGE-SPECIFIC MONEY PAGE EVENTS

1. cta_click_book_consultation
Tracks clicks to /book-consultation.

Parameters:
- cta_location
- destination_page
- service
- page_type

2. cta_click_start_healing_journey
Tracks clicks to /contact.

Parameters:
- cta_location
- destination_page
- service
- page_type

3. related_blog_click
Tracks links from service pages to supporting blog posts.

Parameters:
- event_label
- cta_location
- destination_page
- service

4. internal_link_click
Tracks strategic internal links to related service/support pages.

Parameters:
- event_label
- cta_location
- destination_page
