FAQ

Questions About Contacting Get Real Therapy

What happens after I submit the contact form?

After you submit the form, the practice can review your inquiry and follow up about consultation availability, fit, and next steps. The form is a first step, not a commitment to ongoing therapy.

Do I need to know which service I want before reaching out?

No. You can share what has been feeling hardest lately, and a consultation can help clarify whether anxiety therapy, trauma therapy, depression therapy, relationship counseling, life coaching, or another support pathway may fit.

Is the contact form for emergencies?

No. This contact form is not for emergencies or immediate crisis support. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, use emergency or crisis resources in your area.

Do you offer online therapy in Florida?

Get Real Therapy supports clients in Fort Lauderdale and online across Florida, depending on fit, service needs, and consultation details.
