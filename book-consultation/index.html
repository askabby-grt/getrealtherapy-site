A Gentle First Step

The Consultation Is Here to Clarify Fit

You can use the consultation to share what has been going on, ask questions, and understand what beginning support may look like.

You do not have to disclose everything in the first conversation. The goal is to start with what feels manageable and determine the next best step from there.

A consultation may help clarify:

  • Which service pathway may fit
  • Whether therapy or coaching is the best starting point
  • Questions about pacing, online therapy, and availability
  • Whether the fit feels supportive and grounded
FAQ

Questions About Booking a Consultation

What is a consultation for?

A consultation is a first step to share what you are looking for, ask questions, and explore whether Get Real Therapy may be a good fit for your needs.

Do I need to know which service I want before booking?

No. You can select Not Sure Yet and briefly describe what has been feeling hardest. A consultation can help clarify whether anxiety therapy, trauma therapy, depression therapy, relationship counseling, life coaching, or another support pathway may fit.

Is booking a consultation a commitment to therapy?

No. Booking a consultation is not a commitment to ongoing therapy. It is a way to begin the conversation and determine whether the fit feels right.

Can I request online therapy in Florida?

Yes. You can indicate that you are interested in online therapy in Florida. Fit, availability, and service details can be discussed during the consultation process.

Is this page for emergencies?

No. This page is not for emergencies or immediate crisis support. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, use emergency or crisis resources in your area.
