Welcome

Support that helps you feel more like yourself again

Get Real Therapy is for people navigating anxiety, trauma, depression, relationship stress, emotional overwhelm, and major life transitions in Fort Lauderdale and online across Florida.

Therapy here is not about quick fixes or surface-level coping. It is a space to understand the patterns keeping you stuck, work with your nervous system instead of against it, and move toward a more grounded version of yourself.

If you look fine on the outside but feel overwhelmed, tense, disconnected, or exhausted inside, support can begin before you reach a breaking point.

Approach

A deeper approach to therapy, emotional regulation, and real change

Anxiety, trauma, depression, and relationship patterns are not just mindset problems. They often involve your nervous system, attachment history, emotional patterns, and protective strategies that developed for a reason.

Therapy may include trauma-informed support, EMDR, Internal Family Systems, AEDP-informed emotional processing, and nervous system focused work.

The goal is not to force yourself into change. The goal is to create enough safety, clarity, and support that change becomes possible.

Learn About the Approach What to Expect

The work may support you in:

  • Understanding your triggers and patterns
  • Building emotional regulation and distress tolerance
  • Processing unresolved experiences
  • Softening protective parts that feel extreme or overwhelmed
  • Creating more internal safety, flexibility, and self-trust
Who This Is For

For people who are tired of coping alone

You do not need to be in crisis to begin therapy. You may simply know that what has been working is not working anymore.

You may be a good fit if you:

  • Overthink constantly or feel mentally exhausted
  • Feel disconnected from yourself or others
  • Notice repeated relationship or emotional patterns
  • Feel stuck even though life looks fine on paper
  • Want more than temporary coping strategies
