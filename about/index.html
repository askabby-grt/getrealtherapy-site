Clinical Philosophy

Beyond Surface-Level Coping

My clinical approach is refined, intentional, and deeply personalized. I move beyond surface-level coping strategies, helping clients develop a clearer understanding of themselves and the underlying patterns shaping their experiences.

Therapy is not about labeling you as broken or telling you to simply think differently. It is about slowing down enough to understand what your symptoms, protective patterns, and emotional responses may be trying to communicate.

Through this work, clients can cultivate greater emotional clarity, resilience, and a more grounded sense of control in their lives.

This work is often a fit if you feel:

  • High-functioning on the outside but overwhelmed inside
  • Anxious, disconnected, or emotionally exhausted
  • Stuck in patterns you understand but cannot easily change
  • Ready for deeper work than coping skills alone
  • Curious about EMDR, IFS, or trauma-informed therapy
Training and Background

Grounded in Clinical Training and Continued Growth

I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Florida International University and my Master’s in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Palm Beach Atlantic University.

I remain committed to ongoing professional development and regularly engage in continuing education to stay connected to effective, evidence-based care. That commitment allows the work to stay thoughtful, current, and responsive to the real needs of the people I support.

Internal Family Systems

I have specialized training in Internal Family Systems, an approach that helps clients build a more compassionate and effective relationship with the different parts of themselves.

EMDR Therapy

EMDR can support the processing of unresolved experiences so the nervous system is no longer responding as if the past is still happening in the present.

Trauma-Informed Care

The work honors pace, safety, nervous system capacity, and the protective strategies that may have helped you survive or adapt.

How Therapy Feels Here

Collaborative, Focused, and Deep Enough for Real Change

Therapy with me is collaborative, focused, and results-oriented, while still offering the depth and space required for meaningful, lasting change.

Some sessions may focus on understanding the patterns underneath anxiety, overwhelm, disconnection, or emotional reactivity. Other sessions may focus on regulation, processing, communication, boundaries, or practical next steps.

I guide clients in building a more compassionate and effective relationship with themselves, including transforming anxiety from something to fight into something to understand.

What to Expect Who This Is For

In therapy, we may work toward:

  • More emotional clarity
  • Greater self-trust
  • Less shame around symptoms and patterns
  • A calmer relationship with anxiety
  • More grounded decision-making
  • Healthier ways of relating to yourself and others
